Making Kaya Toast

Ingredients

  • White soft bread
  • Salted butter
  • Kaya jam
  • Eggs
  • Soy sauce
  • White pepper

Recipe

Preparing the eggs

  1. Leave the eggs outside the fridge for 5 minutes as cold eggs may crack
  2. Bring 4 cups of water to a boil and gently lower the eggs
  3. Set a timer for 7 minutes
  4. Use a spoon to lift the eggs and submerge in cold water
  5. Crack the egg and serve with ground white pepper and little bit of soy sauce.

Preparing the bread

  1. Cut the crust off 2 slices of bread
  2. Toast the bread until golden brown. Slightly over toast rather than under toast.
  3. Spread the Kaya jam generously on both sides of the toast
  4. Cut thin slices of cold butter and arrange it to cover one side of the jam.
