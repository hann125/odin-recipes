Making Aglio e Olio

Ingredients

  • Linguine
  • 1/3 cup Olive oil
  • 7-8 cloves of crushed garlic
  • Salt
  • Parmesan
  • Mushroom

Recipe

  1. Boil linguine in salted water until al dente. Reserve a cup of pasta water
  2. Heat a tablespoon of olive oil in a pan and add mushrooms. Do this as the pasta is boiling
  3. Add rest of olive oil and crushed garlic until golden brown. Add reserved cup of pasta water and reduce until emulsifies (2-3 mins)
  4. Add the cooked pasta. Top with grated parmesan.
